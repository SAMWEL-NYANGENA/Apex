Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Apex Robotics Automations ("we," "us," or "our") handles data within the WhatsFlow platform. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Important Compliance: Your use of WhatsFlow is also subject to the WhatsApp Business Policy and the Meta Business Terms.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional WhatsApp automation service, we collect:

  • Account Identifiers: Name, work email, and encrypted login credentials.
  • WABA Credentials: Meta Access Tokens, WABA IDs, and Phone Number IDs. All tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Messaging Content: Text of messages sent and received, timestamps, and message delivery statuses (sent, delivered, read).
  • Knowledge Base Assets: Documents (PDF, TXT) uploaded to train your AI agent. These are converted into searchable vector embeddings.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to:

  • Facilitate official WhatsApp Business API communication.
  • Power your custom AI Chatbot using grounded data from your Knowledge Base.
  • Provide real-time analytics on message delivery and engagement rates.
  • Process bulk campaigns and manage template approvals.

3. AI Processing and Data Privacy

WhatsFlow uses OpenAI’s GPT models to process automated responses. When a customer messages your bot, relevant snippets from your Knowledge Base are sent to the AI model to generate an accurate reply. We do not use customer data or uploaded documents to train public AI models.

4. Data Storage and Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data. Data is shared only with the following infrastructure providers necessary for the service:

  • Meta (WhatsApp): To transmit messages and manage your business account.
  • Supabase: For secure, encrypted database storage.
  • OpenAI: For generating AI-driven conversational responses.

5. User Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your right to be forgotten. You have full control over your data:

  • Account Deletion: You may delete your entire account and all associated data (messages, contacts, and tokens) directly from the "Profile" menu in your Dashboard.
  • Asset Deletion: Individual Knowledge Base files can be removed from the "Knowledge" tab, which triggers an immediate wipe of the associated AI memory.
  • Manual Requests: You can also request a manual data wipe by emailing info@apexroboticsgroup.com.

6. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security protocols, including SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for sensitive API tokens at rest. Access to our backend infrastructure is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

7. International Data Transfers

As a global service provider, data may be processed on servers located in the EU or USA. By using WhatsFlow, you consent to these transfers, which are protected by standard contractual clauses and high-level encryption.
