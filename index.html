What is the WhatsApp Business API?

The WhatsApp Business API is the official Meta platform that allows businesses to automate messaging, integrate systems, and manage customer communication at scale.

Is this different from normal WhatsApp Business?

Yes. The API supports automation, CRM integrations, multi-agent support, AI chatbots, and scalable customer communication systems.

Do I need Meta business verification?

In most cases yes. We help businesses through the Meta onboarding and verification process.

Can I keep my current WhatsApp number?

Yes. Existing business numbers can often be migrated to the WhatsApp API infrastructure.

Can customers speak with AI on WhatsApp?

Yes. AI assistants can answer questions, guide customers, qualify leads, and escalate conversations when necessary.

Is bulk messaging allowed?

Businesses can send approved template messages using Meta-compliant communication practices.
