Frequently Asked Questions

Important details about how the WhatsApp Business API operates for Kenyan organizations.

What is the WhatsApp Business API?

The WhatsApp Business API is Meta's enterprise-grade communication channel. It enables businesses to connect custom software, AI assistants, and automated databases to their WhatsApp number safely.

How is this different from the standard WhatsApp Business App?

The mobile application is intended for single-operator use on limited devices. The official API supports complex automated paths, multi-agent workspaces, bulk marketing compliance, and integration with local CRMs.

Do we need a verified Meta Business Portfolio?

Yes, Meta requires businesses to complete basic portfolio setup. Our technical team guides you through the documentation and verification requirements to ensure a smooth launch.

Can we use our existing company phone number?

Yes. In most situations, an active phone number can be migrated to the official API, provided any existing standard WhatsApp accounts on that number are removed first.

How do AI Agents interact with our clients?

AI models read the customer’s specific questions, referencing your uploaded FAQs, and generate responsive answers. If a query requires human intervention, the system can route the chat to an active agent.

Are broadcast newsletters and alerts permitted?

Yes, you can initiate chats with customers using Meta-approved Template Messages, facilitating notifications, alerts, and promotional announcements in compliance with Meta guidelines.
